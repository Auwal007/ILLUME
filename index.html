Meet Ikedichukwu Peace

ILLUME traces its roots back to 2018, when what is now a growing fashion house began as a simple but deeply intentional vision. Founded by Ikedichukwu Peace, it began with conviction — a belief that fashion could be more than fabric and trends, that it could become a language of identity, dignity, and quiet confidence.
