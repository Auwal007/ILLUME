# ILLUME Bespoke Couture — Client Review & Feedback Sheet

Your satisfaction is our priority. As you review **illume.ng**, use this document to track any edits, copy changes, layout requests, or adjustments you want us to make before going public.

---

## 1. How to Perform the Review

To get the most out of your review, we recommend doing the following:

1.  **Browse on Desktop and Mobile:** Open the site on both a computer and a mobile phone. Luxury clients browse heavily on mobile devices, so checking the fit on a phone is crucial.
2.  **Test All Buttons & Links:** Click through the navigation links, the catalogue detail pages, and click the **WhatsApp Inquire** buttons to verify the messaging flows.
3.  **Submit a Test Inquiry:** Go to the **Contact** page and submit a message. Then, log in to your admin panel and confirm you received the test message.

---

## 2. Review Checklist

Check off these elements as you review the site:
- [ ]  **Spelling & Names:** Ensure all text, names, categories, and designer quotes are spelled correctly.
- [ ]  **Product Details:** Verify that the mock pricing, descriptions, and categories for the featured gowns/suits are accurate.
- [ ]  **Company Ateliers:** Confirm that the locations (Abuja, Ebonyi) and shipping descriptions reflect your actual service structure.
- [ ]  **Social Media & WhatsApp Links:** Ensure the phone numbers and social links in the footer route to your active handles.
- [ ]  **Legal & Policy Pages:** Double-check the **Privacy Policy** and **Terms of Service** pages.
